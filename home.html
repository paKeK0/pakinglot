ABOUT

PARKINGLOT

The project's developers want to make it simple for community members to use our system to request help with parking spots. We thus think about how we may help the community in that area. You can register utilizing websites or apps by completing the application with your basic information. Despite the ease with which modern technology makes it possible to track your whereabouts, this study implies that making use of our project will help to improve and lessen traffic in the area of the community.

">

OBJECTIVE

To lessen the number of incidents in the public community caused by inappropriate parking.

MISSION

We aim to provide a technology system that is efficient and convenient for users.

">

VISION

To enhance the community and reduce traffic congestion.
